# Motivation 

A law of large numbers (LLN) gives conditions under which the average of $n$ random variables converges as $n\to \infty$. 

## Weak vs Strong 

| Strength | Convergence Type               |
| -------- | ------------------------------ |
| Weak     | [[Convergence in Probability]] |
| Strong   | [[Almost Sure Convergence]]    |
